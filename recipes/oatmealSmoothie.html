Oatmeal Smoothie

Description

Ripe bananas and natural peanut butter create a salty-sweet flavor combo in this protein-packed Peanut Butter Banana Oatmeal Smoothie. Itâ€™s perfect for breakfast or any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Banana
  • Milk
  • Peanut Butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Greek Yogurt

Steps

  1. Start your Peanut Butter Banana Oatmeal Smoothie by adding your banana to your blender.

  2. Followed By Milk

  3. Next add your Greek yogurt.

  4. Then add your old fashioned oats.

  5. And your peanut butter.

  6. Finish with a sprinkle of cinnamon.

  7. Put the lid on your blender and blend until smooth, adding ice until you reach your desired consistency.
